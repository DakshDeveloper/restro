The History

Established in 1992, DG & Co. stands as a testament to time, preserving a culinary legacy that has evolved through generations. Steeped in history, this venerable establishment has not only weathered the changing tides but has flourished, embracing cultural traditions that define its identity today.

In 1992, Daksh Gupta, a culinary visionary with a passion for Indian cuisine, opened the doors of DG & Co. with a simple yet profound vision. The establishment quickly became a local gem, attracting patrons with its authentic flavors and warm ambiance. The restaurant's early years laid the foundation for what would become a culinary institution in the heart of Vadodara.
